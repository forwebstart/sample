Kim Jonghyun

Career

  • In 2005, Jonghyun was scouted by SM Entertainment when he was 15 years old, after playing in a song festival with his band at school.

  • In October 2010, Jonghyun was one of twenty idols from various South Korean groups that recorded the song "Let's Go," which promoted the 2010 G-20 Seoul summit. He provided vocals along with labelmates Sungmin of Super Junior, Seohyun of Girls' Generation and Luna of f(x).

  • In June 2011, Jonghyun appeared on KBS' Immortal Songs: Singing the Legend, a television competition program where singers perform new versions of old songs by "legendary singers".

  • n October 2013, Jonghyun debuted as a composer with the track "A Gloomy Clock", which was included on IU's third studio album, Modern Times.

  • After a four-year hiatus, SM the Ballad returned on February 4, 2014, with a new line-up and their second EP Breath. Jonghyun performed the title track "Breath" as a collaboration with Girls' Generation's Taeyeon.
